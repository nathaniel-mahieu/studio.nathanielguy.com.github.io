Colorful Porta Potties

These porta potties were found at Cornerstone Music Festival in Illinois 2006. They actually didnt smell too horribly at the time, and weren't dusty from the roads yet. The color is brought out thanks to the sun and lack of dust. Cornerstone porta-potties are definitely the most colorful I have seen. They were lined up and gave many campers relief that week. These particular bathrooms were behind merch tent 3, and had not been covered in band posters. Because they were lined up, the leaning one adds a bit of interest to the picture, the lines add some movement, and all together liven up the photo. More...
