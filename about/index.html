My Photography

I began taking pictures in the summer of 2005 and found I had a slight talent for it. Seeing as I enjoyed it I kept taking them just for fun. Walking in the woods was something I had always enjoyed, adding a camera made it more interesting. After about a year of that I started looking at ways to make my pictures better, read a couple of articles and looked at those "artistic traits". I am by no means an artist, almost the oppisite. I am quite linear, and math oriented. But aside from that; Most of my photography is nature oriented. This is mainly because the most abundant subject around where I live is nature. Beautiful.
